There were 16 Mahajanapadas capital means where the king/the ruler, the protector of boundary and people and their assets used to stay so if Kamboja capital is Rajapura that means its king lived in Rajapura and the treasury/kosh containing assets of the people particularly their produce was also kept in capital in king's protection.
Uss zamane mein transport nahi tha. ek jana Capital se 10-20 km door gaon mein rehta hai wo roz King ke warehouse se din bhar ka khana nikalne Capital nahi jayega. so Har kisan apne ghar mein mitti ke bade matkon mein apna Daily Use ka anaj rakhta tha and send surplus to king.
Agar unka gaon loot liya jaye ya jal jaye toh unhe pata tha ki "Hamara kuch anaj King ke paas safe hai" aur Wo ek tarah ki Fees thi jise King apne soldiers ko salary dene ke liye use karta tha.
