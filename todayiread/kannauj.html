Jaichandra of Kannauj

Kanyakubja ka arth Wahan jahan kanyaein kubji/jhuki hui hain. Raja Kushanabh ki 100 betiyan thi. Ek din jab wo upvan mein tehel rahi thi, tab wahan se Vayu Dev guzre aur unke samne vivah ka prastav rakha. Unhone kaha ki kisi devta ki patni banne ke bajaye wo apne pita dwara chune gaye kisi manav se vivah karna zyada pasand karengi. Is inkaar se Vayu Dev krodhit ho gaye. Unhone un sabhi kanyaon ko shraap de diya ki wo "kubdi" (bent/hunched) ho jayengi.
Jaichandra of kannauj/kanyakubj, uttar pradesh.
people of kannauj don't consider him gaddar.
when ghori attacks prithviraj jaichandra doesn't send his army to help because he wasn't called
2 years after killing prithviraj ghori attacks kannauj thereafter jaichandra dies fighting ghori
